Our Insurance Practice Areas

Car Rental Insurance Class Actions

We target unfair and deceptive charges in the car rental industry and investigate potential insurance fraud affecting both United States and United Kingdom citizens.

  • Promised but undelivered supplemental liability insurance
  • Uninsured motorist insurance gaps
  • Hidden or illegal fees
  • Deceptive insurance coverage practices

Property Damage Insurance Claims

We pursue class actions against insurance companies that systematically underpay or deny legitimate property damage claims, ensuring policyholders receive full compensation.

  • Total loss vehicle underpayments
  • Sales tax recovery on insurance claims
  • Tag, title, and dealer fee recovery
  • Systematic claim undervaluation

First Party Insurance Disputes

We champion consumers' rights in first party insurance disputes, identifying patterns of wrongful denials and underpayments across the insurance industry.
